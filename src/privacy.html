PRIVACY POLICY

How we handle your data

Last updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address. This is necessary to process and fulfill your orders.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate order updates and confirmations
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our products and services

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL). We implement appropriate security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. We do not store credit card or payment details — all payments are cash on delivery.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to deliver your order (e.g., courier services) or comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at s7sportspk@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

We use local storage for cart and wishlist functionality. No tracking cookies or analytics scripts are used on this site.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, reach us at s7sportspk@gmail.com.
